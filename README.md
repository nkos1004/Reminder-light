# Reminder-light

A smart Bluetooth-connected flashing light powered by ESP32 that helps you stay on top of your tasks and to-do's with visual notifications.

## Overview

Reminder-light is an IoT device that pairs with your phone via Bluetooth to provide visual reminders for your tasks and to-do items. Instead of just relying on sound or vibration, it uses an eye-catching flashing light to get your attention, making it impossible to miss important reminders.

## Features

- **Bluetooth Connectivity**: Seamlessly connects to your smartphone via Bluetooth for easy task synchronization
- **Visual Notifications**: Distinctive flashing light patterns to alert you to important reminders
- **ESP32-Based**: Built on the reliable and popular ESP32 microcontroller platform
- **Task Management Integration**: Receive reminders for your tasks and to-do items directly on the device

## Hardware Requirements

- **ESP32 Microcontroller** - The core processor
- **LED/Light Component** - For the visual reminder notifications
- **Power Supply** - (Battery or USB power)
- **Optional Components** - (Bluetooth modules if not using built-in ESP32 Bluetooth)

## Getting Started

1. **Hardware Setup**: Assemble the ESP32 and LED components according to the project specifications
2. **Software Installation**: Flash the firmware to your ESP32
3. **Pairing**: Enable Bluetooth on your ESP32 and pair it with your smartphone
4. **Configuration**: Set up your task reminders in the companion app or on the device
5. **Start Using**: Receive visual notifications for your reminders
